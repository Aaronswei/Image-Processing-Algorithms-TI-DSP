PROGRAMMING:
0) START
1) Read Image from Flash
2) Check pixel belongs to which range 
3) Apply equation of line accordingly and calculate pixel value of corresponding O/P image.
4) Send Pixel Value
5) Goto next pixel, 
6) Repeat step 2-5 for entire image
